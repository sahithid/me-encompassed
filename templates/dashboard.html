PhysicalTracker

The physical tracker tracks you physical activity and the amount of time said activity is done.



Emotional Tracker

The emotional tracker tracks your mood in three basic mood ranges. Sad vs. Happy, Calm vs. Angry, and Bored vs. Excited. These are the basic building blocks to your emotions at any given time or day.



Intellectual Tracker

The intellectual tracker tracks your productivity and how long you've used your brain.



Social Tracker

The social tracker tracks your social wellbeing in terms of your social interaction. It asks a set of questions and gives a score based on the answer. The higher the social score, the more social interaction you had at a given day.



Spiritual Tracker

The spiritual tracker is a simple measure of how long you have been spiritual at a given time or day. It tracks mindfulness.
